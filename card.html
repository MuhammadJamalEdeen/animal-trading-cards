Cheetah

Cheetahs are the world's fastest land animal, capable of reaching speeds of up to 70 mph.

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average Length: 1.3 meters
  • Average Lifespan: Item #3
  • Habitat: 11 years

The Cheetah is a tall and elegant cat in appearance. Large chest, narrow waist, long thin legs, and a slim well muscled build. This animal was definitely made for speed.
